Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I sign up as a Teacher or Student?

    To sign up as a teacher/Student, do the following:

    Step 1: Visit the Sign Up Page

    Step 2: Enter your Full Name, Email, Phone Number, and Password

    Step 3: Choose either Student or Teacher from the dropdown

    Step 4: Click Sign Up to continue

  • Where is the dashboard?

    To view the dashboard, you need to Log In.

  • How do I edit my Profile?

    To edit your profile, you need to Log In.

    You will be directed to your dashboard where you will see a link to your profile page

  • How do I view my classes?

    To view your classes, you need to Log In.

    You will be directed to your dashboard where you will see a link to view your classes
